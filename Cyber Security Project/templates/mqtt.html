💡 Why is this dangerous?

In retail IoT, MQTT brokers without authentication allow anyone to publish messages. An attacker can send an 'UNLOCK DOOR' command, leading to physical theft or unauthorized access to the store.

Impact: Physical Breach, Inventory Theft.

🛡️ SOC Recommendations

  • Vulnerability: Unauthenticated MQTT broker & plain-text payloads.
  • Risk: HIGH. Lack of message integrity verification.
  • Mitigation: Enforce TLS Encryption and HMAC-SHA256 signatures to ensure only trusted sensors can control hardware.
