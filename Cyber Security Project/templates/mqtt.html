How it works

Store sensors (fridge temperature, shelf weight, entry door) talk to a central controller over MQTT. We simulate two versions side by side:

  • Insecure: no auth, no TLS, plain payloads. Attacker can spoof messages.
  • Secure: username/password + TLS 1.3 + HMAC-SHA256 signed payloads.
